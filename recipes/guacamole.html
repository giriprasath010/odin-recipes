Guacamole

Description

Guacamole is one of my all time favourite go to snack. It is very simple to prepare and you don't need any special ingredients. Most probably you'll find the ingredients in your household. Go through this page completely, I have shared the entire procedure for preparing my favourite snack.

Ingredients

  • Avocados
  • Lime
  • Salt
  • Diced onion
  • Tomato
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt.
  2. Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper.
  3. Refrigerate 1 hour for best flavor, or serve immediately.

There you go! Thats how guacamole is made. I hope you like this recipe well.
